Facebook Messenger Redesign Concept

The Research

User Surveys

In the starting stages of my research, I conducted a 20 question survey that targeted the main functions of the Messenger app and received 10 responses, all giving insight on their experiences with the app. From those responses, I was able to draw possible solutions that would help me enrich the user's experience.

Defining the Problem

Ideation

Wireframes

I created wires to best ideate how this would've been executed on a low fidelity level. These collection of screens encompass the solution to user pain points with the overall goal to enrich the user's experience within the app.

Prototypes

Next Steps

Now that I have designed prototypes, my next steps would be to get user feedback through user testing, allowing each person to interact with the design and seeing if there are gaps that need to be filled.

Conclusion

Facebook Messenger, being a very popular app, has a large turnout of users who are satisfied with the product. However, there is always another way to look at something to make it even better. I noticed that even a trending application doesn't meet the needs of all its users (It's not possible). But, being able to collect user's perceptions and thoughts makes it easier to satisfy the greater mass of a userbase and make their experience that much more great.
